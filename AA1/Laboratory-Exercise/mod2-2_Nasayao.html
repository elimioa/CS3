Fruits Basket

Tohru, a poor high school girl neglected by her family, discovers a family of humans that can turn into animals according to their zodiac sign. Since her grandfather's house was under construction, she resulted to live in the woods which was owned by the Sohma family. After some time, a member of the Sohmas, Yuki - who is also her classmate - discovered that she was living in their estate; and because of pity, he invited her to live with the Sohmas. She would then later on know the secrets of the Sohmas and the rest is history. This anime is suited for those interested in romantic comedies, slice of life, and supernatural fiction types of series.
