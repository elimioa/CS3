Mango Sorbet Recipe

This recipe is good for one person and has a prep time of 4-12 hours. Best served immediately after making it.

Ingredients and Materials needed:

  • 1-2 Ripe mangoes
  • 1 Lemon
  • Blender or food processor
  • Container
  • Freezer
  • Knife
  • Spoon

Procedure:

  1. Peel the mango and cut out its seed. Then, slice it into 1-inch thick cubes.
  2. Store in a container and freeze it for at least 4 hours.
  3. Once done freezing, take it out and put into blender/food processor. Pulse while adding the juice of one lemon (more or less)
  4. Pulse until no large chunks can be seen or until you've reached a smooth and desired texture
  5. And you're done, enjoy!
